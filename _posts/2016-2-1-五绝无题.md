---
title: 五绝无题
key: 2016.2.1
tags: 五绝
categories: 律诗
---

<blockquote class="blockquote-center">玉树万方皓，
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">孤家一寸辉。
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">东西友无影，
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">前后雪花飞。
</blockquote>

</blockquote>
