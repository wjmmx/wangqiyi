---
title: 七绝冬至祭坟
key: 2018.12.19
tags: 七绝
categories: 律诗
---

<blockquote class="blockquote-center">又临冬至祭黄花，跪拜感恩烟如霞。
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">香火飞天落地雨，丝丝成泪湿孤家。
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center"></br>
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">七律参加二中老三届
</blockquote>
