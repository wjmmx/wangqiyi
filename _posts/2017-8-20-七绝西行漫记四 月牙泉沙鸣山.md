<blockquote class="blockquote-center">山聚鸣沙闻涛声，泉凝静月看海平。
</blockquote>
<blockquote class="blockquote-center">大漠戈壁少色彩，天降绿洲龙点睛。
</blockquote>

</blockquote>

</blockquote>

</blockquote>
